Competition Go

This is an app that uses flutter and google firebase. I got this idea from a friend, because of a problem he noticed. He noticed when he would street race it was hard to tell when someone would jump off the line to early or who would win. This app registers users and will allow a person to input start and ending gps coordinance. Racers will show up to the start coordinance and when countdown is finished they will launch. The winner will be chosen by who reaches the ending gps coordinace first, using the phones gps and accelerometer, IF they did not red light. This is still a work in progress.
